Whats good video

1. Journey video
	- Show real process
	- Talking without scripts
	- Show everything, not just the perfect result with perfect editing
2. Building in Public
	- Point of view yourself as someone in the process of building something
	- Show your mistakes, your point of view, your experience, etc
3. Expert-based video
	- You as 'expert'
	- Teaching specific knowledge / niche
	- Your `'basic knowledge'` -> can be that will solve problem for specific user
